Nitte Mahalinga Adyanthaya Memorial Institute of Technology, Nitte

Nitte Mahalinga Adyanthaya Memorial Institute of Technology (NMAMIT), established in 1986, is an autonomous engineering college located in Nitte, Karnataka. It is affiliated with Nitte University and recognized by AICTE, New Delhi. Accredited with an "A+" grade by NAAC and several programs accredited by NBA, the institution offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, and computer applications. Known for its excellent infrastructure, research facilities, and strong industry collaborations, NMAMIT fosters innovation, entrepreneurship, and holistic student development. The campus provides state-of-the-art labs, hostels, sports, and cultural activities, ensuring quality education and professional growth in a vibrant learning environment.
